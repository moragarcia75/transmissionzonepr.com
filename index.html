Our Specialized Technical Capabilities

While standard auto repair centers focus on simple exterior parts replacements, our shop is built around advanced internal drivetrain components. We diagnose, calibrate, and rebuild.

01

Complete Transmission Rebuilds

Full internal tear-down and rebuilds of automatic and manual transmissions. We replace worn planetary gear sets, clutch packs, gaskets, and seals with extreme precision.

02

Hydraulic Valve Body Service

A clean workbench environment is essential to clear delicate valve body channels. We calibrate pressure parameters to avoid delayed shifts and hydraulic line failures.

03

Advanced Diagnostics

We use specialized computer diagnostic scanning tools to read electrical solenoid blocks, identifying system fault codes accurately before beginning mechanical labor.

04

Torque Converter Refurbishment

Addressing slipping, shuddering, or complete lock-up failures with precision alignment tools to restore seamless power transition.

05

FWD & RWD Drivetrains

Versatility in handling transverse transaxles in modern passenger sedans as well as traditional longitudinal drivetrains on utility pickup trucks.

06

Solenoid & Electronic Repairs

Modern transmissions rely on electrical actuators. We replace faulty shift solenoids, wiring harnesses, and control sensors to bring shift patterns back to standard.
